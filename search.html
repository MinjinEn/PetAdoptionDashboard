Check out adoptable pets in organizations within your city!

If the city you entered has pet rescue organizations then it should show some statistics about their adoptable pets. There should be a donut chart with each slice representing each organization by its organization ID. When hovered, the organization ID and the counts of adoptable pets available will be shown. In addition, information about breeds and pet types will be represented in bar graphs.
